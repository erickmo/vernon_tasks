Reports Domain

Owner: Erick Mo  |  Status: Active

Scope

Mobile + portal reporting hub. Aggregates analytics, OKR rollup, sprint velocity, risk, leaderboard, workload, and overdue tasks. Two surfaces:

  • /portal/reports — desktop exec dashboard (3 tabs: OKR, Sprints, Team).
  • /m/reports — mobile hub (3 cards: My Reports, Projects I Manage, My Team).
